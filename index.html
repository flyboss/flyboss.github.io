I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Liyao Xiang. Prior to that, I received my M.S. degree in Software Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Beijun Shen. I earned my B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Tongji University.

My research interests include privacy and security, with a particular focus on leveraging program analysis and large language models to detect privacy leakages and code ownership issues.

I am actively seeking job opportunities in Shanghai, where I can contribute to solving challenging problems in the fields of program analysis and large language models.
